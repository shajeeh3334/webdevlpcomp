M-DIT

In a short span of time, the Institute has carved a niche in the field of technical education in the state through its advanced courses in engineering and technology and has become one of the most preferred institutions for aspiring students in the state. Students at M-DIT enjoy the twin advantage of quality education and serene environment, at the picturesque hinterlands of the campus located at Ulliyeri, Kozhikode.
